Projects

Knots by Kimi Logo

Knots by Kimi, founded by Kim Huynh, is a small, charming crochet business based in Calgary, Alberta, offering creations such as handcrafted flower bouquets, accessories, home décor, and more. I created a logo that reflects her identity, personality, and passion for crocheting. The resulting design is a blend of minimalism and playfulness, capturing Kim's warm personality and creativity.

Hatshepsut Website Prototype

This is a website prototype for Hatshepsut, a fictional all-natural vegan makeup brand. Tasked to strictly follow the brand identity provided by my Visual Communications Instructor, I developed a user-friendly, functional, and aesthetically pleasing website flow that showcases the brand's ethos and products effectively.

Valentine's Day Card

Relationship or not, Valentine's Day is one of my favorite holidays to celebrate. This February, I crafted a Pokémon-inspired Valentine's Day card for my partner. Considering his favorite Pokémon character, I illustrated it and assembled everything together in Canva to create a card that captured his interests and delivered a short yet sweet message.

Personal Brand Identity

For a Visual Communications final project, I developed a personal brand identity. Drawing inspiration from the personal touch of a signature, I created a logo based on my own. By condensing it into a mark that is simple, minimal, and memorable, I conceived the idea of a single stroke representing the first and last initials of my name.

Infographic Poster

For a Visual Communications assignment, I created a vintage-looking and fun infographic that brings awareness to the benefits of thrifting, including affordability, supporting local communities, sustainability, and unique finds.

Petro Floor Business Card

For a client, I redesigned their logo and business card inspired by a floor plan sketch. Focusing on recognizable and differentiating qualities, I created a simple, clean, geometric logo that is both unique and eye-catching.
